Ingredients

  • 1 Bag - Frozen Edamame in Shell
  • 1 Tbsp - Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp - Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp - Mushroom Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp - Chili Garlic Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp - Stock
  • 1 tsp - Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp - Onion Powder
  • 1 dash - White Pepper
  • 1 dash - Chinese Five Spice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring 1-2 cups of water to a boil in a steamer. Once boiling add the edamame.
  2. In a small sauce pan combine all other ingredients over very low heat.
  3. Steam the edamame until cooked to desired texture. Approximately 8-10 minutes.
  4. Once cooked, remove the edamame and add to a mixing bowl. Toss with the sauce.
  5. Serve immediately.
