Ingredients

  • 1 Head - Green Kale
  • 6 Cloves - Garlic
  • 3 Tbsp - Stock
  • 2 Tbsp - Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice cloves of garlic lengthwise into 1/8th inch thick pieces.
  2. Destem the kale and rip into 2-3 inch sized pieces.
  3. Add oil and garlic to a wok or large pan and heat on low.
  4. Let the garlic infuse in the oil for five minutes. Then turn the heat up to medium-high.
  5. Add the kale and toss with the garlic oil.
  6. Add the stock and cover with a lid for two minutes.
  7. After two minutes stir the kale and recover with the lid for another minute.
  8. After one minute remove the lid and stir until stock has mostly cooked off.
  9. Cook until kale is desired softness and season with salt.
  10. Serve immediately.
