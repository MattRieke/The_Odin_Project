Ingredients

  • 1 Block - Firm Tofu/li>
  • 2 Tbsp - Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Scallions
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 inch Ginger
  • 1 Tbsp - Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp - Stock
  • 2 Tbsp - Szechuan Chili Oil
  • 1 Tbsp - Mushroom Sauce
  • 1 dash - White Pepper
  • Salt
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Drain the block of tofu and place on a cutting board. Cover with another cutting board and 2 pounds of weight. Let rest for 30-60 minutes.
  2. While tofu is draining, slice scallion whites into 1 inch pieces and scallion greens into two inch pieces.
  3. Mince garlic and ginger until paste like.
  4. Drain tofu of expelled liquid then slice into 1 inch x 1 inch x 1/2 inch pieces.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large fry pan until shimmering. Fry the tofu pieces over medium-high. Turn halfway through and fry until golden.
  6. Combine stock, szechuan chili oil, mushroom sauce, and white pepper.
  7. Once tofu is fried, remove from the pan and set on a towel to remove excess oil.
  8. Add sesame oil to pan and turn heat down to low. Add minced garlic and ginger to oil. Fry for 3 minutes to influse the oil.
  9. Turn the heat up to medium and add the whites of the scallions. Fry for two minutes.
  10. Turn the heat up to medium high and add the tofu, sauce, and scallion greens. Toss until greens are wilted.
  11. Plate and garnish with toasted sesame seeds and cilantro. Serve immediately.
